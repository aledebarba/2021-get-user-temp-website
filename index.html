SETTING THE STANDARD FOR THE NEXT LEVEL OF LASH & BROW TRAINING

We provide the most comprehensive quality training to lash & brow artists everywhere. We strive to empower our students to provide the best lash & brow services in their local area, build a brand they are proud of, and become savvy business owners.

Lash and brow treatments are the fastest growing beauty movement this decade and there is no sign of this slowing down. Becoming a lash and brow technician could be the right move for you whether you are looking for a refresher course, adding to your existing beauty qualifications or are interested in a career change into the beauty arena. No matter what your background you could become a successful professional lash & brow artist! What are you waiting for? Train with us and get the industry insights and tools to be your own boss!

Suzanne Egan Academy has extremely high standards and believes that certification is earned, not purchased. To become certified, all work will be thoroughly assessed by the Suzanne Egan Academy to ensure students graduate with confidence and meet the highest of industry standards.
